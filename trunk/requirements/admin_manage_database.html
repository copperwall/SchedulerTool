2.2 Managing the Database

The administrator manages the database by launching the administrator program and clicking an item under the Databases dropdown towards the top of the screen. The administrator accesses and edits the instructor, course, and location databases through the items in the dropdown. See Figure 2.1.1-1.

When the administrator clicks the Instructors button, the instructor editing page appears. (2.2.1)
When the administrator clicks the Courses button, the course editing page appears. (2.2.2)
When the administrator clicks the Locations button, the location editing page appears. (2.2.3)
