3.2 Qualitative System Characteristics

Security and Privacy

Use CAS to authenticate users based on their Cal Poly ID. This makes sure that unwanted users can't gain elevated priveleges.

EmplID's will never be displayed with names.

Simplicity vs. Power

The Scheduler features a spectrum of simplicity and power. Students and instructors are able to simply vote and record their preferences, while the admin has the freedom and flexibility to adjust settings to meet the needs of the department. While the Scheduler boasts both simplicity and power, it tends towards the simple side of the spectrum whenever possible.
