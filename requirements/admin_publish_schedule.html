2.6 Publishing a Schedule

The administrator sees this dialog after clicking Publish in the menu bar.

Figure 2.6-1: The publish confirmation dialog not filled

The administrator fills out where they want to publish the schedule.

Figure 2.6-2: The publish confirmation dialog filled.

After clicking the Publish button, the system publishes the schedule to the web for all students and instructors to view, designated at the given URL. In this example shown in Figure 2.6-2, the system publishes the schedule at http://users.csc.calpoly.edu/~copperwa/projects/work/schedule/Winter_2014_3.

Clicking Cancel closes the dialog without publishing the schedule.
