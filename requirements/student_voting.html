2.7.3 Student View

When a user clicks the Voting button toward the top of the page, the following page appears.

Depending on whether the voting window is open or closed, this page either allows students to vote on classes they would like to take for a particular term or allows students to view their vote. Each vote has a course and department drop-down. After making their selections, students submit their votes via the Submit button. Once a student clicks either the Submit or Cancel button, the scheduler application returns to its main view.

Figure 2.6.3-1 Voting while the voting window is open. Note notice of when voting window closes.

The Add another vote button under the set of votes allows the student to add more class votes.
The student initially sees only one vote option. After he clicks the Add another vote button, another vote option appears.
