1.5.1 Related Systems: FindMyShift

Findmyshift is an online employee scheduline system. Its major selling point is its mobility. The fact that it is a web based application means any end user anywhere can generate and view schedules from any internet-connected computer.

Although the mobility of findmyshift is a much desired feature, it does not offer automatic schedule generation - one of the goals of the Scheduling Tool.

    Good Features:
  • Can be accessed from anywhere.
  • Last minute staffing changes and shift swaps can be dealt with at any time, anywhere.
  • Up-to-date employee schedules are always available for managers and their staff, whenever they're needed.
  • Staff can check, request and cancel shifts without phoning in.
  • Built-in shift notifications - staff get alerts when the schedule changes.
  • Alerts and general notifications can be sent via email, text message or push notification.
  • Point and click scheduling.
  • Intuitive right click menus.
  • Direct on-screen schedule editing.
  • Drag and drop interface.
  • Vacation management.
  • iCal integration
  • PDF, XLS, or HTML export of schedule
  • Facility view - shows shifts by location
    Bad Features:
  • Proprietary, paid application.
  • The interface is fairly complicated.
  • The information for each schedule block is crowded and confusing.
  • The only view is a weekly calendar.
    Missing Features:
  • Automatic schedule generation.
  • Daily shift view.
  • Staff availibility constraints and preferences.
